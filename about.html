Who am I?

Hello, world! I'm Gurvir Chahal—a student, programmer, snowboarder, and more. I enjoy listening to podcasts and keeping myself moving through activities such as weightlifting and swimming.

I've always found it challenging to express myself, and I hope this website becomes a space where I can do just that.



My Journey

Like many people, I had no idea what I wanted to do after high school. I enjoyed math and numbers, so I chose accounting. I spent two semesters in the program before realizing it wasn’t for me. Instead of dropping out right away, I stayed in accounting but took a few unrelated courses, including an intro to computer science and psychology class. That’s when I was introduced to C++, my first programming language. I quickly fell in love with coding, drawn in by the creativity and innovation in the field. Seeing what others had built inspired me to pursue computer science, leading me to transfer into SFU’s Computer Science program—and I haven’t looked back since.
